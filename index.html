CiteCraft - Instant Citation Generation for Google Docs

CiteCraft is a Google Docs add-on that helps users generate accurate and instant citations directly within Google Docs. Our tool uses public metadata from CrossRef to automatically generate citations in various formats (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.) based on the documents you're working on.

Purpose of the App:
The primary purpose of CiteCraft is to simplify the citation process for researchers, students, and academics by allowing them to generate citations seamlessly while writing within Google Docs. By connecting with CrossRef's public database, CiteCraft ensures that users can access reliable and up-to-date citation information for scholarly articles, books, and more. This tool is designed to save time and enhance productivity by eliminating the need for manual citation entry.
